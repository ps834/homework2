The Pop King - Michael Jackson


The greatest education in the world is watching the masters at work. - Michael Jackson


Micheal Joseph Jackson was born on 29th August 1958. He was a very famous break-dancer, singer and songwriter. He was titled 'The King of Pop' and was the best-selling artist even at the time of his death.


Some interesting facts about Michael Jackson:

  • He is the Most Awarded Artist of All Time.
  • The first music video by a black to be aired on MTV was Billie Jean.
  • He is the Highest-Earning Deceased Artist.
  • Thriller is the best selling album of all times.
